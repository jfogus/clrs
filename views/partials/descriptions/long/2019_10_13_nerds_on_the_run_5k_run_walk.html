Crazy Legs Race Series is proud to present the Nerds on the Run: 5k Run/Walk benefiting Lakeshore Public Media. All runners and walkers will receive one-size-fits-all gloves and a hat. The first 50 individuals that sign up will receive a pair of PBS Nerd sunglasses and a Lakeshore Public Radio water bottle. The course will be a mixture of roads and trails.

Lakeshore Public Media, a nonprofit and community-licensed media organization, brings Northwest Indiana and the Chicagoland quality PBS, NPR, and original programming that informs, inspires, and entertains. The organization strives to be the recognized media resource for citizens of all ages to experience lifelong learning, celebrate human diversity, and engage in civic concerns.

Lakeshore Public Radio is Northwest Indiana's own listener-supported public radio station featuring the best of NPR, as well as unique region-focused programming.

Lakeshore PBS broadcasts a unique blend of national and locally-produced programming broadcast to more than 14 counties across Indiana and Illinois.

For more information about Lakeshore Public Media, please visit www.lakeshorepublicmedia.org.
