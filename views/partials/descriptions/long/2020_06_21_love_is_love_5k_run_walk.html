This race will be held at the beautiful Bluhm County Park in Westville, IN. It will be on the pavement bike trail across the street from the park shelter. Part of the proceeds will benefit the NWI Pride Events, Inc. Please visit their website at www.nwi-pride.org or their Facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/nwiprideevents/. Come celebrate diversity, acceptance, inclusion, and individual expression of truth.
