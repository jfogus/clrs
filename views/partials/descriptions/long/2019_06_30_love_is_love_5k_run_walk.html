The Love Is Love 5k Run/Walk will be held at the beautiful Bluhm County Park in Westville, IN. This event will be on the pavement bike trail across the street from the shelter in the park. Part of the proceeds benefit NWI Pride Events Inc. Please visit their web site www.nwi-pride.org or Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/nwiprideevents/. Come celebrate diversity, acceptance, inclusion, and the individual expression of truth.
