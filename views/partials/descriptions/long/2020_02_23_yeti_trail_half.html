Come experience the beautiful Bluhm County Park in Westivlle, Indiana. Trails may be muddy, wet, or snowy, so come prepared for the challenge. The course is three loops around the park with one aid station you will hit twice. If you need more aid please carry a hydration pack. This race is low key. All runners receive a winter hat and gloves and a finisher's medal. You have 4 hours and 30 minutes to complete the course. This race is part of the Crazy Legs Race Series.
