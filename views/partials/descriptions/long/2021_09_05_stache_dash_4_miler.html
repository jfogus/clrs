Come experience the beautiful Town of Porter by running this event. This race will be a 2 mile out-and-back course on pavement streets. We will be starting at the community center at Hawthorne Park. I am encouraging you to grow your "stache" out for this event. If you are unable to grow a mustache, you will be provided one. Part of the proceeds will benefit the Porter Parks and Recreation Department. The race is on a Sunday so to not interfere with races occuring on Labor Day.

All registered individuals will receive a "Stache" Trucker Hat.
