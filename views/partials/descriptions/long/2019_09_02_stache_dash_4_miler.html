Come experience the beautiful Town of Porter by running this event. This race will be a 2 mile out and back course on pavement streets. We will be starting at the Community Center at Hawthorne Park. I am encouraging you to grow your "stache" out for this event. If you are unable to grow a mustache I will provide you one. Part of the proceeds will be benefitting the Porter Parks and Recreation Department.

Each runner will receive a one-size-fits-all winter hat and gloves
