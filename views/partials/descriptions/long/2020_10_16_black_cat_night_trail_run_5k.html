In honor of National Feral Cat Day, supporters from around the world are advocating for the lives of cats and educating their communities about humane policies, like Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR), that help save cats’ lives and protect all cats in all communities. The Crazy Legs Race Series is proud to host the Black Cat Night Trail Run 5k. Part of the proceeds will be donated to Alley Cat Allies.

Alley Cat Allies is the global engine of change for cats. We protect and improve cats’ lives through our innovative, cutting-edge programs. We are seen around the world as a champion for the humane treatment of all cats. We work toward a world where every cat is valued and protected, and every community and shelter has policies and programs to save their lives. Please check out their website at https://www.alleycat.org

Headlights are mandatory for this event and will be available for purchase on race day if needed for $10 (batteries included). Runners will receive a t-shirt.
